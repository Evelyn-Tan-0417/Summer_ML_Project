Project

Build a tool that uses before-and-after meal photos to estimate food waste, learn a person’s waste patterns, and recommend better portions or leftover strategies.

Input: user photos of their meal before and after eating
Output: recommendation of a better portion for next time

Potentially ask the user:
How hungry were you before eating? 1–10
How much did you like the meal? 1–10
Why did you leave food? (ex. too full; didn’t taste good; portion too big; too much carb; too much vegetable; texture issue; no time)

The program will compare the two photos and estimate 
food left (proportion; ex. 33% of rice left, 10% of veggies left)
percent wasted
likely food categories wasted
estimated calories wasted
estimated protein/carbs/fat wasted
CO2/foodprint estimate 

How to tell the leftover
Could use pixel to see how much % is left from the original meal

Comparing percentage to serving size:
You use an estimated serving size.
Ex. Typical rice portion = 180g, Leftover percentage = 33%, Estimated rice wasted = 180g × 0.33 = 59g
